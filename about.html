About me

I am a cyclist! Although I have been doing it for just 2 years, I identify as one. I give my all to the things that I love to do. As I have decided on a path for cycling, I have done so for computers as well.

I have been working with computers since the day I could no longer play a certain video game on my parents computer. I wondered why and I found out the graphics card wasn't good enough. It was early in high school. I saved up for it and did my research. Soon I found myself fiddling with all different aspect of computing, eventually landing myself in the high school's computer program.

My passion only grew from then on and after testing the water in many aspects of computing, I have decided to study for DevOps. I am currently on a track to complete System Administration and proceed to DevOps. I am enjoying my experience as a developer.
